# RoboArt-Squad
## Automatic rangoli maker

**Preface**
In India we grew up seeing beautiful colourful vibrant rangoli’s, right outside during  every festivals in several home. Specially in our Villages we would see our relatives make Rangoli’s on a daily basis. When our Grandma’s & mothers used to  make rangoli during festivals, we always tried to help them. But often lost patience to do such a fine art, it was very time consuming & challenging indeed!! 
But those beautiful colours, patterns and gave us such a good feeling. And we also learnt that Rangoli’s are  also considered  a part of good luck, positive energies, and to have ants and small insects eat it outside our home and don’t enter our homes!! All this made us wonder why we could not make it daily outside our homes, just like in many of our Villages? 
That’s how we came up with idea of a Rangoli RoboArt Squad!!  We chose this project because we want to make sure we don’t lose this beautiful traditional art, that also serves an ecological function.  Just because we can’t do it or get busy!! 

**The Robotic Solution**
Our robot is an XY plotter built with 3D-printed parts and a microcontroller. It works like this:
•	The Robot's Job: It draws the base design on the floor with perfect precision, saving us from the tiring work.
•	The User's Job: You feed the Rangoli powder into the machine and use an app to pick/draw a design.
•	Smart Features: The robot’s app has AI that suggests cool ideas for the user to implement, such as adding diyas or flowers, to make your design look even better.

**Real-Life Impact**
If people start using our robot, making beautiful Rangoli’s and enjoying this art form, can become a daily habit, instead of a rare almost dying festive task. And families can keep the tradition alive at a reasonable cost.
Why This Project Is Important
This project is about reviving an almost fizzling part of Indian culture. It uses technology to fix the "intricate, complex artistic hard work", so that people enjoy the beautiful art in their homes daily and stay engaged with their culture. By making art easier, we ensure that Rangoli remains a vibrant part of our homes for generations to come.
